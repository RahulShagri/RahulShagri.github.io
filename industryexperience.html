Innovation Hub Management Co-op

Kinectrics Inc. (Former Ontario Hydro R&D)
Toronto, Ontario
August 2022 - August 2023

As part of the central team, the Innovation Hub, I have played a key role in developing business cases for the Boston Dynamics SPOT robot for remote inspection and maintenance of nuclear power plants. I successfully led drone demonstrations for nuclear and non-nuclear inspections, prioritizing safety through Job Safety Analysis (JSA) and Pre-Job Brief (PJB) protocols. My work also included in-depth analysis of nuclear licensing and regulations, researching potential revenue opportunities from national laboratories, and developing a software to monitor New Product Development (NPD) projects' progress and revenue forecasts.

Research and Development Intern

Curiouz TechLab
Manipal, India
August 2019 - December 2019

Our objective was to evaluate challenges doctors face during tumour removal using endoscopic scissors for cancer research. We designed a specialized endoscopic scissors to address these issues, employing Siemens NX for CAD modelling. We successfully patented the innovative endoscopic scissors design.

Patent Published - June 17, 2021 - “A scissors-needle system for intra-cavitary hydro-dissection and excision of tissues” - WO2021116776

Research and Development Intern

MLBE Laboratory, Cracow University of Technology
Kraków, Poland
June 2019

The Małopolska Energy-Efficient Building Laboratory is a 6-storey laboratory built for research on low-energy buildings for a sustainable future. As part of the internship, I assisted in designing and analyzing a heat exchanger using Autodesk Fusion360 and Ansys. The goal was to transfer heat from hot kitchen air to cold water, providing heated water for restrooms, thus saving energy.
